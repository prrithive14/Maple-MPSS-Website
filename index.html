Why Maple MPSS 02 / 04

The advantages of an offshore operation, without the cost of building one.

Most of the economic reality facing Canadian manufacturers is structural. We work on that layer.

Large manufacturers have spent decades building engineering and manufacturing capacity in India, Mexico, and Southeast Asia. That capacity is what lets them quote aggressively, absorb design work in-house, and compete on lead time. Mid-sized firms have been locked out of that model — the capital requirement, the timeline, and the operational distance are too much to take on.

Maple MPSS is that capacity, run as an operating partner. We handle the engineering in India, the manufacturing in India, and the client relationship in Canada. You send a drawing or a part. We send back a quote, a shipment, and a Canadian project manager who owns the outcome.

POINT 01

A Canadian relationship

Every engagement has a Canadian project manager responsible for quoting, communication, revisions, and delivery. You deal with Burlington.

POINT 02

Offshore cost structure

Engineering and manufacturing run out of Chennai. That cost structure shows up directly in what we can quote, without surcharges stacked on top.

POINT 03

Overflow capacity on call

When your own shop is bottlenecked, we take the overflow. Contract work, handled like our own — drawings in, parts out, Canadian PM on the account.

POINT 04

One team, two services

Sourcing and software are run by the same operating team. An audit that uncovers a software bottleneck is handled by people who can actually build the fix.
