What Works Best 02 / 02

A concrete part beats a capability call.

OPTION 01

A drawing

PDF, DWG, or STEP. Even an old or unclear drawing is enough — cleaning it up is part of what we do. Attach it to the email after you submit the form.

OPTION 02

A part description

A photograph of a sample, a sketch, or a written description. We'll reply with questions and a proposed drawing. No engineering on your side required.

OPTION 03

An OEM quote

If you're buying a replacement part from an OEM and suspect you're paying a premium, send us the quote and whatever drawing or sample you have. We'll tell you honestly whether it's worth re-sourcing.

OPTION 04

An operational bottleneck

For AI services: describe the bottleneck or workflow you'd automate in one or two paragraphs. If it's a fit for an audit, we'll say so. If it's narrower than that, we'll scope it directly.
